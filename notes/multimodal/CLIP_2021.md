## 2. One-Sentence Summary

- **Core idea**: Contrastive learning is an efficient way to learn visual representations from natural language supervision and enable flexible zero-shot transfer.

---

## 3. Problem and Main Idea

- **What problem are they solving?**
  - Close the gap between vision learning and language-scale supervision.
  - Learn from practically unlimited raw text instead of only limited gold labels.
- **What is the main idea?**
  - Pretrain on large-scale image-text pairs and classify by matching images with text prompts.
- **Why does it matter?**
  - It enables zero-shot transfer and connects visual representations directly to language.
