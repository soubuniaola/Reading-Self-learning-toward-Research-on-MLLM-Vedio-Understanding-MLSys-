## 3. Problem Setting

- **What problem are they solving?** 
    - Scale gap between natural language model and vision model; 
    - middle ground between learning from a limited amount of supervised "gold-labels" and learning from practically unlimited amounts of raw text
- **Why does this problem matter?** 
    - limits model's zero-shot capabilities
- **What was the common approach before this paper?** 
    - weak supervision 
    - fine-tuning on the transfer benchmarks
- **What limitation of prior work are they targeting?**

---

## 4. Main Contributions

1. Close the gap and study the behaviors of image classifier trained with natural language supervision at large scale.
2. Create a new dataset with 400 million (image, text) pairs.
3. Learns to perform a wide set of tasks (OCR, geo-localization, action recognition...)
4. More computationally efficent than task-specific model while outperforming it in ImageNet
